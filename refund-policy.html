refund policy

We have a 30-day return policy, which means you have 30 days after receiving your item to request a return. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition you received it, unworn or unused, with tags attached and in the original packaging. You will also need your receipt or proof of purchase. To initiate a refund, you can contact us at PARALLAXCLUB@GMAIL.COM. If your return is accepted, we will send you a return shipping label as well as instructions on how and where to ship your package. Items sent to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted. You can always contact us with any questions about returns at parallaxclub@GMAI.COM.

damage and defects

Please check your order upon receipt and contact us immediately if the item is faulty, damaged, or if you received the wrong item so we can assess the problem and fix it. You can always contact us if you have any questions about your order:parallaxclub@GMAI.COM.

exchange

The fastest way to ensure you get what you want is to return the item you have and, after accepting the return, make a separate purchase for the new item.
