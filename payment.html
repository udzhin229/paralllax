payment

When placing an order in the online store, only online payment is possible. Payment on delivery is not available. Available payment methods: by bank card, by telegram bot An electronic receipt for orders paid by non-cash method is sent to your e-mail immediately after payment. The electronic receipt contains all the data about the completed payment transaction. In case of an order creation error: Refunds are made by the Seller within 10 days. The operation for crediting funds by the issuing bank can take up to 30 calendar days.
